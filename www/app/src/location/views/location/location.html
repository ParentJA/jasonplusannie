The Location

The wedding and all related events will take place in the vibrant Penn Quarter neighborhood of Washington, DC. The neighborhood is very walkable and mere blocks from the Chinatown/Gallery Place metro station and from Metro Center. For your convenience, we’ve reserved a room block at the venue, the Hotel Monaco, but there are also many other hotels accessible to the area.

Travel

Washington National Airport (DCA) is the most convenient airport to the city. It is connected directly by the Metro’s yellow line to the Chinatown/Gallery Place metro station.

Baltimore/Washington International Airport (BWI) is linked to downtown Washington, DC by both Metro bus and MARC/ AMTRAK trains. Washington Dulles Airport (IAD) is linked to downtown Washington, DC by the Silver Line Express bus and Metro bus line 5A
